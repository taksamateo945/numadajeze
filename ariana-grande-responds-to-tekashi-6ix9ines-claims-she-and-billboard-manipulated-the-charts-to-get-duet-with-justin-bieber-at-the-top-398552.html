Ariana Grande Responds To Tekashi 6ix9ines Claims She And Billboard Manipulated The Charts To Get

Tekashi 6ix9ine accused Ariana Grande as well as Billboard of manipulating the charts! After all, how could her and Justin Bieber’s ‘Stuck with U’ collab be number one and not his ‘Gooba’ track?

The singer was quick to clap back at the rapper on social media, their beef only growing amid explosive back and forths.

As fans may know, Ari and Justin’s Stuck with U collab song debuted at the very top of the Billboard charts, right above Gooba, the song Tekashi dropped after getting released from prison.

He argued that Ari’s single had a massive last hour sales boost, accusing Billboard of manipulating the results.

She did not hesitate to shoot back and so did Justin at some point!

Ariana responded to the claims against her but did not mention the rapper’s name even though it was obvious she was talking about his accusations.

Judging by her response, she was also a bit confused as to why she, as a woman, was the only one being attacked by Tekashi despite the song in question being a collab with Justin Bieber.

The artist wrote: ‘I would like to address a few things which i don’t usually do (this has gone a bit too far)…my fans bought the song. JUSTIN’S fans also bought the song. OUR fans bought the song (never more than four copies each, AS THE RULES STATE). they’re ride or die motherf***ers and i thank god every day i have them in my life. not only when they fight for us to win but because they are some of the greatest people i know.’
