Stalker Suspect Served With Restraining Order From Singer Brandy

Brandy successfully received a restraining order against a young woman who showed up at her home without an invitation. The Blast was the first to report that the singer filed a civil harassment restraining order against a 21-year-old woman via the LA County Courts.

The 21-year-old girl has repeatedly shown up at Brandy’s home over the last few months, leaving Brandy with no other option but to take it to court.

On the 29th of Monday, the police apprehended the woman and a spokesperson for the LA Sheriff’s Department confirmed she had been detained. Authorities booked her on suspicion of felony stalking, and she’ll have to pay a $150,000 for her release.

If the 21-year-old unidentified woman is convicted of the charges, she may have to face a year in jail. According to Hot New Hip Hop, the suspicion of a felony stalking charge constitutes a crime in which a suspect follows, harasses, or stalks another person with or without the intention of inspiring fear in the targeted person.

Brandy currently lives in a gated community like many other celebrities in Los Angeles. However, she doesn’t know who the woman is, and is fearful to see her there whenever she arrives back home.

Brandy isn’t the only person who has dealt with a stalker or a home invader in recent months. For celebrities and other highly visible people, it’s not uncommon for them to become targets for mentally ill people or other people who may have malicious intentions.
