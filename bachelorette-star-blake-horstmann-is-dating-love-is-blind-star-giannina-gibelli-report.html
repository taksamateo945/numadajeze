Bachelorette Star Blake Horstmann Is Dating Love Is Blind Star Giannina Gibelli (Report)

It’s a reality TV romantic crossover event!

The Bachelorette‘s Blake Horstmann and Love Is Blind‘s Giannina Gibelli are dating, People reported.

Click inside to read more…

“They’re taking things slow, but [they] seem really happy together,” a source told the outlet.

The 32-year-old and 28-year-old reality TV stars met recently, and have been enjoying spending time together according to the report.

While they haven’t gone official on social media, fans have also noticed the two appearing in the same venues recently.

Days ago, Blake posted a picture of himself at Mile High Spirits Distillery in Denver with the caption: “2022 is gonna be the best year in a while” followed by a winking emoji. Giannina later posted a picture from the distillery’s barrel room, writing: “repeat after me: I am about to walk into the best year of my life.”

Blake Horstmann was on Becca Kufrin‘s season in 2018, and went on to become the runner-up. He also revealed on Bachelor in Paradise that he had hooked up with some of the contestants before filming, and he quit just weeks into the show.

Giannina appeared on Love Is Blind in 2018 and got engaged to Damian Powers, but they never married. She revealed she was single again last summer.
