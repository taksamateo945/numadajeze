Five films we think you need to see this year

2015 has brought with it a whole host of interesting and dynamic films, we’ve had: Avengers: Age of Ultron, Antman, The Kingman’s Secret Service, Mad Max: Fury Road; the list truly could go on but the point of this piece is to tell you all the glorious cinematic wonders that still have yet to come…

5. Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation

Release Date: 30th July

Directed by: Christopher McQuarrie

Cast: Tom Cruise, Jeremy Renner, Simon Pegg, Alec Baldwin, Ving Rhames, Rebecca Ferguson

Synopsis: Ethan Hunt assembles his team for their final and most difficult mission—to prove the near-mythical organisation, otherwise known as The Syndicate’s existence and bring the organization down by any means necessary.

Tom Cruise…hanging onto a plane…whilst it’s taking off- what can’t that man do?

4. The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 2

Release Date: 19th November

Directed by: Francis Lawrence

Cast: Jennifer Lawrence, Josh Hutcherson, Liam Hemsworth, Woody Harrelson, Elizabeth Banks, Sam Claflin

Synopsis: After the catastrophic events of Mockingjay Part One, Katniss Everdeen has set her sights locked on liberating the citizens of Panem, by any means necessary.

Judging by the trailer, this final Hunger Games instalment is set to have battles by the bucket load!

3. Fantastic Four

Release Date: 6th August

Directed by: Josh Trank

Cast: Miles Teller, Michael B. Jordan, Kate Mara, Jamie Bell, Toby Kebbell, Reg E. Cathey

Synopsis: When four people teleport to an alternate universe,  their physical form alters thus granting them with new abilities, which they must learn to harness and work together as a team to save the Earth from a certain Doctor.

We’re going right back to the start to rework one of Marvel’s classics…

2. Spectre

Release Date: 6th November

Directed by: Sam Mendes

Cast: Daniel Craig, Christoph Waltz, Monica Bellucci, Léa Seydoux, Naomie Harris, Ralph Fiennes

Synopsis: A cryptic message, sent to MI6, sets in motion events that will see James Bond come face-to-face with the sinister organisation known as SPECTRE.

Bond’s back again, and looking sleeker than ever, in what’s set to be an action-packed hit!

1. Star Wars: Episode VII – The Force Awakens

Release Date: 18th December

Directed by: JJ Abrams

Cast: Harrison Ford, Carrie Fisher, Mark Hamill, Anthony Daniels, Peter Mayhew, Kenny Baker

Synopsis: The Force Awakens is set approximately 30 years after the events of Return of the Jedi, and features new leads Finn, Rey, and Poe Dameron alongside our much beloved characters returning from previous Star Wars films.

It’s what we’ve all been waiting for…
