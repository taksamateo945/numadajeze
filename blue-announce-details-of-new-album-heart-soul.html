Blue Announce Details of New Album 'Heart &amp; Soul'

00’s pop legends Blue have delighted fans around the world today (April 11th) with the news they are to release their 6th studio album ‘Heart & Soul’ later this year!

Serving as the follow up to 2015’s ‘Colours’, the record is scheduled to drop Friday, September 9th and will be available on CD, Vinyl and Cassette, as well as on all digital platforms.

Taking to social media, the boys shared their excitement with fans by posting a video with the message: “It’s happening! We are please to announce our sixth studio album “Heart & Soul” is now available for pre-order.”

Alongside news of the albums release, the group (made up Antony, Duncan, Lee and Simon) have also revealed the rescheduled dates for their postponed UK tour, which is now set to take place this coming December. Tickets for all shows are on-sale now, whilst tickets purchased previously will remain valid for the new dates.

Unfortunately due to prior commitments, Atomic Kitten can no longer make the new tour dates, so fans are encouraged to keep a close eye on all of Blue’s socials (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter and Facebook) where news of replacement special guests will be announced soon.

Check out the rescheduled dates in full below:
