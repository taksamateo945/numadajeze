Shawn Mendes To Perform on The Graham Norton Show

Fresh from making his UK festival debut with a stellar live set at Radio 1’s Big Weekend in Hull on Sunday May 28th, it has now been officially confirmed that Shawn Mendes will be performing on the hit BBC 1 talk show The Graham Norton Show.

The 18-year-old Canadian worldwide superstar will be performing his latest massive hit single ‘There’s Nothing Holdin’ Me Back‘ as well as sitting down for a short chat with the Northern Irish host on Friday June 9th. The show is due to be filmed in London on Thursday June 8th.

Shawn will join British boxing star and World Heavyweight Champion of the World Anthony Joshua, and actress Rachel Weisz.

The upcoming live performance will mark Shawn Mendes’ first ever appearance on The Graham Norton Show. The ‘Stitches‘ singer has previously performed on other UK chat shows including The One Show, but he has never appeared on The Graham Norton show until now.

Make sure you catch Shawn Mendes’ eagerly anticipated performance of ‘There’s Nothing Holdin’ Me Back’ on The Graham Norton Show, Friday June 9th, at 10.55pm on BBC 1.
