LeAnn Rimes Celebrates 32nd Birthday in a Bikini With Eddie Cibrian

32 and fabulous! LeAnn Rimes celebrated her 32nd birthday on Thursday, Aug. 28, marking the occasion with a day full of cake, sun, and time with loved ones. 

The country singer started the day on a positive note, tweeting Thursday morning, “It’s official 32!!! #allgoodvibes” and sharing a photo of herself in (what else?) a bikini top, cutoff denim shorts, and a beach cover-up, sipping a green juice.     

“Starting 32 on a healthy note before it goes downhill lol,” she captioned the image. 

The celebrations kicked off early, as Rimes shared midday that her husband of three years, Eddie Cibrian, had presented her with a ring and necklace by jewelry designer Jacquie Aiche. She and Cibrian, 41, also hit the beach, cuddling together in the California sun, on what Rimes said was a “beautiful birthday.”

Surrounded by friends to ring in 32, the reality star’s evening festivities included a party with an impressive multi-layer unicorn-themed birthday cake, adorned with rainbows and oversized candles. “Ohhhhhhhhhhh the unicorns #magical,” Rimes wrote with a photo of the creation via Instagram. 

Rimes was still feeling the love on Friday evening, when she shared a photo of herself and her husband, writing, “I’m truly blessed #leannandeddie.” She went on to add, “I’m sooooo grateful! Crying just cause I’m happy #eddiethinksimcrazy lol.”
